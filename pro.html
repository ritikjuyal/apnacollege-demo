About this app

When an unexpected situation such as an earthquake, typhoon, flood, or other natural disaster, terrorist incident, civil war or political change occurs, the affiliated university or institution registers the safety confirmation target person in advance in the cloud system and the student who set this application You can send a safety confirmation notification to the staff, etc. all at once or select it. The person who installed this app can receive the safety confirmation notification sent by the cloud system from his / her affiliation and return his / her safety and location information promptly to it. You can also directly send and receive messages to and from your affiliated system, or simply tap the button on the app to make a phone call or send an e-mail directly to the emergency contact in your country of residence specified by Iraq Japan.
