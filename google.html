Earn Play Points

Begin earning 1 point for every ₹5 on purchases billed through Google Play. This includes in-app purchases, apps, games, books, subscriptions, and more.

Earn up to 8x points during special events. Get bonus points for trying featured apps and games.

Use Play Points

Redeem your points for discounts and special items like characters, gems, and more in some of your favorite apps and games.

You can also redeem points for Google Play Credit to use on anything, including games, apps, and books.

Level up for more rewards

There are 4 levels. The higher your level, the more points you earn on everything you buy.

Certain levels have access to weekly special prizes that get better with each level.

When you reach a new level, you keep it for the current and following calendar year.
